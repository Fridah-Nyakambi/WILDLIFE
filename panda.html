Pandamiti is a swahili word for "plant trees"

we've compiled a list of effective ways to reduce your environmental footprint. The biggest game changers are labelled "top tips".

The initiative aims to buld a tree planting culture amongst communities and schools in different parts of Kenya. We have so far planted 1 million trees with the support of thr Kenyan government, NGOs,corporates and public donations.

Pandamiti aims to :

  • Develop school gardening for environmental stewardship amongst students and guarantee of fresh foods and agricultural learning.
  • Educate and learn from communities through art and storytelling

our teams

As well as making changes as an individual you can join others to bring about bigger change. Right now communities across the country are fighting climate breakdown. See what's going on in your area

We have worked with over 20 schools and communities and so far planted 1, 000, 0000 trees with the support of thr Kenyan government, NGOs,corporates and public donations.

Pandamiti Project hosts the following activities for students :

  • Art and craft.
  • Storytelling
  • Mentorship
