our identity

Who we are

WildNow is an African orgernization working to sustain the natural world for the benefit of wildlife and its purporse. we are focused on restoring natural habitats, stopping the mass extinction of wildlife, and empowering communities through sustainable ventures.

Changing lifes and ecosystems

our mission

Our goal is to improve the co-dependency of our biodiversity by reducing the rates of human-wildlife conflict, educating and advocating for sustainable urbanization and development.

To achieve this, the organization is committed to:
  • Improving wildlife habitats with the co-existance of communities around the areas .
  • Building Awareness on environmental issues and innovative solutions .
  • Changing the ideology of economic development in Africa with the incorporation of wildlife conserve

our objectives
